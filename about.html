Welcome to my portfolio!

Welcome to my portfolio!​ ​ ​​​I'm Yeganeh, a UX/UI designer based in Vancouver, BC. My journey started with a Bachelor's degree in IT engineering and five years of experience in the tech industry, where I worked in mobile app testing, technical support, and digital content creation. Through these roles, I developed a strong foundation in problem-solving, user research, and digital design. Later, as a supervisor and nail artist, I honed my customer relations skills and gained a deeper understanding of user needs and business goals insights that enriched my approach to UI/UX. This passion led me to enroll in the UI/UX program at BCIT. ​ Now, I focus on creating intuitive, functional experiences that enhance user interactions. I believe in the power of design to solve problems and make digital products more accessible and enjoyable. Excited to collaborate? Let’s create something amazing together!
